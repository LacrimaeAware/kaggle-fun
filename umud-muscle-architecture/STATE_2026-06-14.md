## Error structure (local diagnostics)

Metric = mean of tolerance-normalized MAE over PA/FL/MT, tolerances 6 deg / 12 mm / 3 mm.

- 35-image benchmark, true scale: PA term 0.150 (MAE 0.9 deg), MT 0.084 (0.25 mm), FL 0.519 (+5.8 mm overshoot).
- 19 test-image hand labels vs the shipped submission: PA 0.642 (model reads ~3 deg low), FL 0.656, MT 0.376.
- Model PA mean over 309 = 14.6 deg. Benchmark-truth mean 18.3 deg. Hand-label mean 19.9 deg.

## PA

- Under-read on the test set, approximately uniform. A flat +2 deg corrects the global offset: 0.58910 -> 0.55075.
- +3 and the high-end-weighted hinge both regressed. The under-read is not concentrated at high PA; per-image reshaping fits the 19 labels and fails on the hidden 309.
- The 19-label proxy predicted the +2 result and mispredicted +3 and the hinge. Usable resolution is coarser than ~0.02. Not usable for fine PA tuning.
- Output-level PA correction is exhausted at +2.

## Muscle

- 140/309 muscles read from burned-in UI text (12L3 family = VL; Lumify family = VL/RF/GM; one Tri Surae = GM). 169 carry no text.
- Per-muscle model PA: GM 22.8 deg, RF 17.4 deg, VL 15.1 deg. RF > VL is anatomically inverted (RF should be lowest).
- CNN muscle classifier: 1.0 validation accuracy on text-bearing images, fails on the no-text family (its no-text "GM" predictions carry hand-label PA of 10-14 deg). Input included the overlay region; it learned the text, not the anatomy. No information beyond the OCR.
- Muscle-conditioned PA correction (toward literature means, and via the classifier) is worse than +2 on the proxy or not transferable. Rejected.

## FL

- FL = MT/sin(PA); truth correlation 0.97 on both benchmark and hand labels. FL is determined by MT and PA, not independent.
- Identity-FL (MT/sin(PA)) scores worse than the current fragment-extrapolation FL. No post-hoc FL lever.
- Residual FL error after MT (solved) and PA (corrected) is per-image extrapolation variance.

## Scale

- MT, which is pure scale, is the lowest-error term on the test set (0.376). Not the bottleneck.

## Ruled out this session

- Post-hoc PA reshaping beyond a uniform shift.
- Muscle-conditioned PA correction (OCR or classifier).
- Post-hoc FL correction.
- Scale as the current bottleneck.
